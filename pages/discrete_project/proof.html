Prove: If global temperatures are rising then climate change is real.

T=temperatures are rising

C=Climate change is real

Prove: T → C

Proof by Contradiction

Contradictive Statement: T → ~C

First we prove that temperatures are rising.

This graph proves that the average global temperature is rising.

T being true means that according to our contradictory statement, climate change is not real.

However, as defined by NASA, climate change is a long-term change in the average weather patterns that have come to define Earth's local, regional and global climates. Climate refers to the long-term (usually at least 30 years) regional or even global average of temperature, humidity, and rainfall patterns over seasons, years, or decades.

This directly contradicts our assertion that climate change is real therefore proving that climate change is real.
